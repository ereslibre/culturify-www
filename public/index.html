Culturify
Because culture matters.
 

What ?

Culturify is an effort to preserve our own freedom.

It is a project that tries to keep open things open. Through a thourough design we allow you to access to culture in a completely legal way.

Composed by a client and a server, both ad-free it provides a really easy way to share links with others, allowing you for instance to share a set of links on a really interesting study by a well-known University.

Both ends are obviously free software. GPLv3 licensed.

You can run your own instance of Culturify platform easily. Stay tuned for news !

Why ?

Access to culture should always be free. Culture should be shared by every individual in the world, and that is what makes us advance. Trying to block sharing is exactly the same as trying to block progress.

Additionally, it seems that some lawyers still do not realize this cannot be done.
